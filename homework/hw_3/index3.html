TAG

[tæg]
noun
  1. a label attached to someone or something for identification
    "he gave his pet a collar with a metal name tag"
    synonyms: label, ticket, badge, mark, marker, tag, tally, sticker
  2. a small piece attached to a main body
verb
  1. attach a label to
    "mothers suspected babies were wrongly tagged"
    synonyms: label, attach, mark, identify
  2. add something as an afterthought
    "she meant to tag her question at the end"
    synonyms: add, attach, append
